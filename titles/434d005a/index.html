The FMV Intro and cutscenes Run at full speed but, the screen margins are misaligned from the motion blur filter, making the in-game cutscenes blurry.

Sound:
-----------------------
The audio sounds fine.


Performance / Gameplay:
-----------------------
This game runs with heavy slowdown running at 5fps(30fps game) during the game's tutorial. It was too slow to test any actual missions. Also seem to cap at 20 fps which I am not sure why? https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x9BsUlUfI1o


Graphics:
-----------------------
The game suffers from minor graphical glitches. Firstly aforementioned burry cutscenes. Other than that, I could only see some minor misaligned textures.

Overall, due to performance issues and my laptop suffering from a CPU bottleneck, and other issues with the frame cap, I cant mark this game as playable.
